About The Bark Avenue Inn

Welcome to The Bark Avenue Inn! We are dedicated to providing top-tier care, luxury, and comfort for your furry friends. Founded on the belief that pets are family, our facility offers a boutique boarding and daycare experience tailored to your dog's needs. From cage-free accommodations to personalized activities, every detail is crafted with love and care.

Located in the heart of Nassau County, NY, we’re proud to be a home-grown, WOOF-certified facility. Our professional team is here to ensure your pets feel at home while you’re away. Join our community of happy pet owners and see the difference in our care.
